How to Play:

  • • SPACEBAR: Jump (press twice for double jump)
  • • ARROW DOWN or S: Crouch/Slide under obstacles
  • • Collect coins: Bonus points and power-ups
  • • Avoid obstacles: You can take 2 hits before the monster becomes deadly
  • • Watch out for gaps: Falling into gaps ends the game instantly
  • • Monster: Gets closer with each hit - avoid it after 2 obstacles!

Power-ups:

  • • Shield: Block one obstacle hit without dying
  • • Magnet: Pulls in nearby coins automatically
  • • Boost: Rockets forward with auto-dodge
  • • Double Coins: Doubles coin rewards briefly
